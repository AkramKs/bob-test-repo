# Cinnamon Rolls Recipe

A classic, soft, and gooey cinnamon rolls recipe perfect for breakfast or dessert.

## Ingredients

### For the Dough:
- 1 cup (240ml) warm milk (about 110°F/43°C)
- 2 ¼ teaspoons (7g) active dry yeast (1 packet)
- ½ cup (100g) granulated sugar
- 2 large eggs, room temperature
- ⅓ cup (75g) unsalted butter, melted
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour (plus more for dusting)

### For the Cinnamon Filling:
- ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter, softened
- 1 cup (200g) packed brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg (optional)

### For the Cream Cheese Frosting:
- 4 oz (115g) cream cheese, softened
- ¼ cup (60g) unsalted butter, softened
- 1 ½ cups (180g) powdered sugar
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1-2 tablespoons milk (as needed for consistency)

## Instructions

### Step 1: Activate the Yeast
1. In a small bowl, combine warm milk and 1 tablespoon of sugar from the measured amount.
2. Sprinkle the yeast on top and let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy and bubbly.

### Step 2: Prepare the Dough
1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the remaining sugar, melted butter, eggs, and salt.
2. Add the activated yeast mixture and stir to combine.
3. Gradually add the flour, 1 cup at a time, mixing with a wooden spoon or dough hook until a soft dough forms.
4. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for 6-8 minutes until smooth and elastic.
5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with plastic wrap or a clean towel, and let rise in a warm place for 1-1.5 hours, or until doubled in size.

### Step 3: Make the Filling
1. In a small bowl, mix the softened butter, brown sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg (if using) until well combined. Set aside.

### Step 4: Assemble the Rolls
1. Punch down the risen dough and turn it out onto a lightly floured surface.
2. Roll the dough into a 12x18-inch rectangle (about ¼-inch thick).
3. Spread the cinnamon filling evenly over the dough, leaving a ½-inch border along the long edges.
4. Starting from the long side, tightly roll the dough into a log.
5. Cut the log into 12 equal slices (about 1.5 inches each) using a sharp knife or unflavored dental floss.
6. Place the rolls in a greased 9x13-inch baking dish, spacing them slightly apart.
7. Cover and let rise again for 30-45 minutes, until puffy and nearly doubled.

### Step 5: Bake
1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Bake the cinnamon rolls for 20-25 minutes, until golden brown on top.
3. Let them cool in the pan for 5-10 minutes before frosting.

### Step 6: Make the Frosting
1. While the rolls are cooling, beat together the cream cheese and butter until smooth and creamy.
2. Gradually add the powdered sugar, beating until combined.
3. Mix in the vanilla extract and enough milk to reach a drizzling consistency.
4. Spread the frosting generously over the warm cinnamon rolls.

## Tips
- **Make ahead:** Prepare the rolls through step 4, then cover and refrigerate overnight. In the morning, let them sit at room temperature for 30 minutes before baking.
- **Storage:** Store leftover cinnamon rolls in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 days, or refrigerate for up to 5 days.
- **Reheating:** Warm individual rolls in the microwave for 15-20 seconds for that fresh-baked taste.

## Enjoy!
Serve these soft, buttery cinnamon rolls with a cup of coffee or a glass of cold milk. Perfect for holiday mornings, brunch, or anytime you need a sweet treat!
